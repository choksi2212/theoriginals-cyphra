Cost Optimization Strategy

Lean Operations Principles

  • Remote-First: No expensive office lease, co-working only when needed
  • Outsource Non-Core: Use freelancers for design, content, legal
  • Open Source Tools: Minimize software costs with free alternatives
  • Cloud Efficiency: Optimize infrastructure costs, use spot instances
  • Performance Marketing: Focus on high-ROI channels only

Scaling Assumptions

  • Headcount: 2 people (Y1) → 3 (Y2) → 5 (Y3) → 8 (Y5)
  • Marketing Efficiency: CAC decreases as organic growth increases
  • Infrastructure: Scales sub-linearly with users (local processing)
  • Gross Margin: Improves from 91% to 97% as we scale

Key Cost Drivers

  • Personnel: 57-63% of expenses (largest cost)
  • Marketing: 21-29% (critical for growth)
  • Infrastructure: 5-6% (stays low due to local processing)
  • Operations: 10-11% (lean admin overhead)
