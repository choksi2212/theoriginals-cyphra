Exit Options & Scenarios

Strategic Acquisition

Probability: 60%

Timeline: Year 5-7

Valuation: ₹15-25 Cr

Potential Acquirers:

  • Password managers (LastPass, 1Password)
  • Cybersecurity firms (Quick Heal, K7)
  • Identity platforms (Okta, Auth0)
  • Indian IT companies (TCS, Infosys)

Rationale: Technology acquisition for biometric capabilities

Private Equity / VC Exit

Probability: 20%

Timeline: Year 4-6

Valuation: ₹10-15 Cr

Potential Investors:

  • Indian VCs (Sequoia, Accel)
  • Cybersecurity-focused funds
  • SaaS-focused investors
  • Strategic corporate VCs

Rationale: Growth capital for expansion

Bootstrap & Hold

Probability: 20%

Timeline: Indefinite

Valuation: N/A (Cash flow)

Strategy:

  • Sustainable profitable growth
  • Dividend distribution
  • Long-term value creation
  • No external pressure

Rationale: Maintain control and independence

Potential Strategic Buyers

Password Managers

Examples: LastPass, 1Password, Dashlane, Bitwarden

Rationale:

  • Add biometric layer to existing products
  • Differentiate from competitors
  • Enhance security offerings
  • Acquire proprietary ML models

Fit: Excellent - Direct product synergy

Cybersecurity Firms

Examples: Quick Heal, K7, Seqrite, McAfee India

Rationale:

  • Expand into authentication market
  • Add to security suite
  • Leverage existing customer base
  • Indian market expertise

Fit: Good - Complementary offering

Identity Platforms

Examples: Okta, Auth0, Ping Identity, ForgeRock

Rationale:

  • Enhance MFA capabilities
  • Add behavioral biometrics
  • Expand into consumer market
  • Technology acquisition

Fit: Excellent - Strategic technology fit

Indian IT Giants

Examples: TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL

Rationale:

  • Add to product portfolio
  • Offer to enterprise clients
  • Build security practice
  • Acqui-hire talent

Fit: Moderate - Strategic but different focus

Exit Preparation Roadmap

Y1-2

Foundation Building

  • Achieve product-market fit
  • Build initial customer base
  • Establish brand presence
  • Clean financial records
  • IP protection (patents, trademarks)
Y3-4

Growth & Profitability

  • Achieve profitability
  • Scale to 4,000+ paying customers
  • Build repeatable sales process
  • Strengthen team & operations
  • Begin strategic conversations
Y5-7

Exit Execution

  • Engage investment bankers
  • Prepare data room & due diligence
  • Approach strategic buyers
  • Negotiate terms & valuation
  • Close transaction

Key Success Factors for Exit

Strong User Base

  • 1M+ total users
  • 20K+ paying customers
  • Low churn (<5% monthly)
  • High engagement metrics
  • Strong NPS (>50)

Proprietary Technology

  • Patented algorithms
  • Unique ML models
  • Clean, documented code
  • Scalable architecture
  • No technical debt

Financial Performance

  • ₹2.5 Cr+ ARR
  • 40%+ net margins
  • Positive cash flow
  • Predictable revenue
  • Strong unit economics

Market Position

  • Clear differentiation
  • Brand recognition
  • Strategic partnerships
  • Competitive moat
  • Growth potential
