Metric Definitions

Key Formulas

  • LTV (Lifetime Value): ARPU × Average Lifespan × Gross Margin
  • CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): Total Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers
  • CAC Payback Period: CAC ÷ (ARPU × Gross Margin)
  • Magic Number: Net New ARR ÷ Sales & Marketing Spend
  • Rule of 40: Revenue Growth Rate (%) + EBITDA Margin (%)
  • Net Revenue Retention: (Starting ARR + Expansion - Churn) ÷ Starting ARR
  • Monthly Churn: Customers Lost ÷ Starting Customers
