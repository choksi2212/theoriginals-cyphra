Risk Analysis

Honest Assessment of Challenges

Major Risks

🔴 Market Risk (HIGH)

Issue: Indians won't pay for password managers

Mitigation: Ultra-low pricing (₹29/month), focus on B2B

🔴 Competition (HIGH)

Issue: Free alternatives everywhere (Google, Bitwarden)

Mitigation: Unique biometric auth, privacy-first positioning

🟡 Acquisition Cost (MEDIUM)

Issue: CAC of ₹1,333 vs ₹29/month pricing

Mitigation: Organic growth, content marketing, viral features

🟡 Retention (MEDIUM)

Issue: Indians churn fast from paid subscriptions

Mitigation: Strong onboarding, continuous value delivery

🟢 Technical Risk (LOW)

Issue: Technology already built and working

Mitigation: N/A - this is our strength

🟢 Funding Risk (LOW)

Issue: Only need ₹8.5L to start

Mitigation: Bootstrap-friendly, no VC dependency

Worst Case Scenarios

Scenario 1: Nobody Pays (60% probability)

  • Get 10K free users but <50 paying customers
  • Burn ₹8.5L in 3 months
  • Pivot to B2B or shut down
  • Loss: ₹8.5 Lakhs

Scenario 2: Slow Growth (30% probability)

  • Get 100-200 paying customers in Year 1
  • Revenue ₹2-4 Lakhs, expenses ₹10 Lakhs
  • Need seed funding or shut down
  • Loss: ₹10-15 Lakhs

Scenario 3: Base Case (10% probability)

  • Hit projections: 150 paying in Year 1
  • Break even by Year 3
  • ₹1 Cr profit by Year 5
  • Success!
