Key Revenue Assumptions

Conversion & Retention

  • Free-to-paid conversion: 1.5% (Year 1) → 2.0% (Year 5)
  • Pro tier monthly churn: 8% (improving to 5% by Year 5)
  • Pro tier annual churn: 25% annually
  • Annual plan adoption: 40% of Pro users
  • Average customer lifetime: 12 months (Pro monthly), 24 months (Pro annual)

Market Penetration

  • Total addressable market (India): 5 Crore password manager users
  • Serviceable available market: 50 Lakh (willing to pay)
  • Target market share by Year 5: 0.4% of paying market
  • Chrome maintains 65%+ browser market share
  • Passwordless authentication adoption grows 30% annually

Pricing Assumptions

  • Pro tier pricing remains stable at ₹29/month
  • Annual pricing remains at ₹199/year (43% discount)
  • No price increases planned for first 3 years
  • Enterprise pricing (if launched): ₹99/user/month minimum 50 users
  • No major competitive pricing pressure expected
